Keyword Instructions

Use the following keywords and line-breaks to format your test cases.
(All fields are required. Keywords are not case sensitive.)

  1. "Test that" - One line naming your test case.
  2. One or more lines of description in any format.
  3. "When" - One or more lines describing a single step instruction.
  4. "Then" - One or more lines describing the expected result of that step.
  5. Repeat 3-4 as needed for multiple steps, or begin a new test case with "Test that".
